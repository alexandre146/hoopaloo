Register Student

To register students in the system access the URL http://dataccc.gmf.ufcg.edu.br/hoopaloo/register_student/. Other way of realize this is cliking at the link Register Students in Users menu (1).

Register is made following: one student by line and each line is composed by studentID, username, e-mail and complete name (2). The studentID should have 8 digits and be unique.
